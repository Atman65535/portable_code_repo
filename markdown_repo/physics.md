$$

### Adiabatic $\partial Q = 0$
$$
\begin{cases}
\partial Q = \frac{i}{2}R \partial T + P\partial V \\
P = \frac{RT}{V} \\
\Rightarrow \frac{\partial T}{T} + \frac{(\gamma - 1) \partial V}{V} = 0
\end{cases}
$$

Then we have
$$
TV^{\gamma - 1} = constant
$$
